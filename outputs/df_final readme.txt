Columns				Explanation
GEOID				County identifier by Federal Information Processing System (FIPS) Codes
n					Twitter - Total tweet volume
hateful_tweets		Twitter - Number of hateful tweets
users				Twitter - User volume
hateful_users		Twitter - Number of hateful users
truncated_tweets	Twitter - Number of truncated tweets. Tweets may be truncated (text clipped to reduced length)
quote_tweets		Twitter - Number of quote tweets
monthly_variation	Twitter - Temporal variation of total tweet volume. Ratio between lowest and highest month.
weekly_variation	Twitter - Temporal variation of total tweet volume. Ratio between lowest and highest week.
hate_interest		Google Trends - Average interest across all hate keywords (see Hohl, A., Choi, M., Yellow Horse, A. J., Medina, R. M., Wan, N., & Wen, M. (2022). Spatial distribution of hateful tweets against Asians and Asian Americans during the COVID-19 pandemic, November 2019 to May 2020. American journal of public health, 112(4), 646-649.)
vics				FBI UCR - Number of victims of anti-Asian hate crimes
